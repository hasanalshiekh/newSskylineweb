Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and plans

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Enterprise customers can also set up custom billing arrangements.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans include a 30-day free trial with no credit card required. You can explore all features risk-free.

What support is included?

Starter includes email support, Professional includes priority support, and Enterprise includes 24/7 dedicated support with SLA guarantees.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.

How do user limits work?

User limits refer to active users who can log in simultaneously. You can add or remove users as needed, and we'll adjust your billing accordingly.
